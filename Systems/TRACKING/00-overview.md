# TRACKING — Output Dashboard

**Single responsibility:** Track everything this OS produces — articles, webinars, stakeholder touchpoints, investment platform milestones.

**Status:** `Active`

TRACKING is the living record of what GRIOT and Carlson have built together. It does not plan. It does not analyse. It records. GRIOT updates it at the end of every session.

At the start of every session (`/session-start`), GRIOT reads this file first to know exactly where things stand — what's been published, what's in progress, who hasn't been contacted, what legal gate is next.

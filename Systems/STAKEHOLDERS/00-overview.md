## Why This Exists

Carlson's value is his relationships. He moves between diplomatic circles, Black American investor networks, African government agencies, and entrepreneurship communities. These relationships are his most important asset.

This sub-system ensures:
1. No relationship falls dormant for want of a follow-up
2. Every outreach is informed by the last touchpoint and the next gate
3. DBIA's institutional relationships are mapped and maintained, not stored in Carlson's head

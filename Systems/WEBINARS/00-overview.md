# WEBINARS — Overview

## Single Responsibility

Prepare Carlson to host the monthly DBIA Diaspora Investor Webinar Series, and extract the content value from each session into the CONTENT sub-system.

## What This Series Is

One webinar per month, July–December 2026. One country per session. Carlson hosts. Each webinar exposes 1–3 vetted, embassy-backed investment opportunities in a specific African country.

**Task reference:** TASK-053

This is not a marketing webinar. It is a deal-origination engine. The extended, organic two-hour webinar (investors would not stop asking questions about transparency and fund safety) proved the demand. This series formalises that demand into a monthly pipeline.

## What WEBINARS Produces Per Session

1. **Country briefing** — economy, key sectors, active opportunities, risks
2. **Opening script** — Carlson's 10-minute opening (why this country, why now, what DBIA brings)
3. **Q&A scaffold** — likely investor questions + Carlson's prepared answers
4. **Closing script** — the call to action (platform registration, next webinar date)
5. **Post-webinar takeaway article** — fed into CONTENT sub-system for publication

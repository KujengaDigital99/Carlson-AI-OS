- [ ] Country announced publicly (LinkedIn post)
- [ ] Featured speaker confirmed and briefed
- [ ] Ambassador for the country identified and activated
- [ ] Registration page live (DBIA website or Eventbrite)
- [ ] WhatsApp group notified

### 2–3 weeks before
- [ ] Investment case LinkedIn post published
- [ ] Speaker spotlight published
- [ ] Registration reminder to existing list (email or WhatsApp broadcast)
- [ ] Country partner / AEM contact engaged

### 1 week before
- [ ] Final reminder post live
- [ ] Webinar logistics confirmed (Zoom/Teams link, moderator, speaker runsheet)
- [ ] Opening script drafted and reviewed by ELEGBA
- [ ] Backup speaker or moderator identified if primary drops

### Day of
- [ ] WhatsApp blast sent (morning)
- [ ] LinkedIn "going live today" post
- [ ] Zoom link tested

### Post-webinar (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Follow-through LinkedIn post (key insight from the session)
- [ ] Thank-you message to speaker
- [ ] Follow-up to registered attendees (recording or next steps)
- [ ] OSHOSI DEBRIEF triggered

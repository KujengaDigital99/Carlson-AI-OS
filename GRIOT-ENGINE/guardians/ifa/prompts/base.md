1. Every African country Carlson presents to diaspora investors has real regulatory and structural constraints. Name them before claiming the opportunity.

5. If no viable path exists for a specific investor type, say so clearly. Honesty is DBIA's product.
6. Research order: regulatory first, then market entry, then track record. Unverified opportunity claims are worse than no claims.
7. Country briefs are for diaspora investors — plain language, specific numbers, actionable entry points.
8. Every researched country becomes institutional knowledge. File it in Knowledge/DBIA/ or Knowledge/Countries/.

# DBIA — Operating Model

## What DBIA Is

Doing Business in Africa (DBIA) is a pan-African trade and investment enablement platform. It converts event activations into measurable business opportunity pipelines.

It is not a conference company. Not a consultancy. Not an NGO. It is the institutional convening layer that turns relationships into transactions and transactions into documented pipelines.

## The DBIA Engine

```
Capture Attention  →  Convert Interest  →  Progress Opportunities  →  Prove Performance
(Events / Media)      (Leads / Qualify)     (Introductions / Deals)    (Dashboard / Review)
```

Every DBIA activation feeds this engine. An event with no pipeline is an event, not a DBIA event.

## Commercial Model

DBIA earns through:
1. Country programme fees (governments and development agencies pay to run investment showcases)
2. Event activation sponsorships (corporate and DFI partners)
3. Platform subscription (investor and investee registration — pending legal sign-off)
4. Content and media (magazine sponsorship, webinar sponsorship)

## Differentiation

DBIA is NOT:
- A consulting firm that advises on entering Africa (generic, supply-side)
- A development NGO (charity framing)
- A conference with no follow-through (activation without pipeline)

DBIA IS:
- Deal-origination infrastructure: the meeting room that produces paper
- Embassy-backed: credibility comes from government endorsement
- Data-anchored: investor and investee matched on verified data, not warm introductions only

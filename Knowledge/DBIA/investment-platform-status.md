## What the Platform Does

A self-serve investment matching platform: investors browse vetted investment opportunities and register interest in specific businesses seeking capital.

**DBIA's grade on investees:** Not Vetted → Basic → Standard → Premium

**Key CTA:** "Register My Interest" — investor signals capital intent toward a specific business.

## FAIS — Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act, 2002

**The core question:** Does the platform constitute "rendering an intermediary service" under FAIS Section 1 and Section 7? If yes, DBIA needs an FSP licence — or the platform must be restructured.

**Two features that escalate risk:**
1. "Register My Interest" CTA
2. Vetting Level grading (Not Vetted → Premium)

**Two defensible positions:**
- **Position A (preferred):** DBIA as marketplace only — not intermediary. Explicit disclaimer on every interest action. Admin-facilitated introductions only.
- **Position B:** Category I FSP Licence from FSCA (3–6 months; Fit and Proper assessment). Only if legal counsel confirms Position A is insufficient.

**Mandatory platform safeguards (either way):**
- Disclaimer: "DBIA does not provide financial advice. This platform is a matchmaking service only."
- Vetting levels relabelled as "DBIA Due Diligence Stage" — not "rating" or "grade"
- All financial data self-declared by investee; not DBIA-verified
- No financial transactions completed through the platform

## POPIA — Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013

**Investor data collected:** Name, email, phone, investor type, investment range, risk appetite, country, focus areas

**Investee data collected:** Company registration, founder details, funding amount, financial stage, business gaps

**Requirements:**
- Privacy notice at registration
- Investor financial data NOT visible to investees
- Investee company/founder personal data not exposed until DBIA due diligence complete
- Data retention policy for inactive profiles
- Cross-border data transfer agreements (if hosting outside SA)
- POPIA Information Officer designated (confirm CEO)

## Cross-Border Dimension

DBIA operates pan-African. Target countries have their own equivalents (Kenya: Capital Markets Act; Nigeria: SEC Rules; Ghana: Securities Industry Act).

**Recommendation:** SA-only launch first to reduce regulatory surface. Expand after legal model is confirmed.
